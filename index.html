Frequent asked question about Flood o' Meter Barangay Maysilo Monitoring System.

1. How long does it take to get fully verified?

Answer. Your number will be fully verified as long as you put the correct OTP provided by the system. 

2. Is this exclusive for Brgy. Maysilo residents?

Answer. Yes, Registration is open only for people residing within the vicinity of Barangay Maysilo, Malabon City.

3. What kind of message will I get?

Answer. You will receive an update about how high the flood is and if it is passable for the vehicle.
