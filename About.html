Pruposed Project.

The Philippines is a flood-prone country. The researchers come up with the idea of having a system that monitors the flood level and sends SMS when water reaches a certain height or level. What makes it more unique is that it is for barangay official use only. The system sends notifications via SMS to update the officials about the water level. The idea of an SMS-based warning system is proposed because mobile phones have become a popular communication device among people all over the world. All mobile phones can communicate because it comprises of a GSM. This system acts as an alarm for responders for possible critical height of flood. The purpose of the system is to help the barangay Maysilo of Malabon, one of the most flooded regions of Metro Manila to be notified by the barangay officials and can take actions immediately to avoid any casualties. There are only a few that have a water monitoring system and this is a big help for the barangay. It makes it a lot easier to receive notification from the system rather than go to the place and monitor the pole where the flood water meter is installed or placed.
